How this works (read once, then ignore)
  1. Fill in the form on the left for your own project — takes about 3 minutes.
  2. Hit Add to board. Your card appears on the right, visible to everyone on the call.
  3. Submitting again with the same name + project updates your existing card instead of duplicating it.
  4. The board doesn't push updates automatically — tap Refresh board (or wait for the 10s auto-refresh) to see new entries from others.
  5. At 14:50 each topic group gets 5 minutes — use the Copy pitch button on your card to grab a ready-to-read summary.
